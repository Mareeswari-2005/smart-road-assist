‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions
How do I request roadside assistance? +
Simply tap the "Request Service" button on your home screen, select the type of service you need, and provide your location. A nearby mechanic will be notified immediately.
How much does the service cost? +
Service costs vary depending on the type of assistance needed. You'll see an estimated cost before confirming your request. Final pricing is determined by the mechanic based on the actual work required.
How long does it take for help to arrive? +
Response times typically range from 15-45 minutes depending on your location and the availability of nearby mechanics. You'll receive real-time updates on the mechanic's arrival time.
Can I track the mechanic's location? +
Yes! Once a mechanic accepts your request, you can track their real-time location and estimated arrival time through the app.
What if I need to cancel my request? +
You can cancel your request anytime before the mechanic arrives by going to your active request and tapping "Cancel Request". Please note that cancellation fees may apply if the mechanic is already en route.
